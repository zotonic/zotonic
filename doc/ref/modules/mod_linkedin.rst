
.. include:: meta-mod_linkedin.rst

The mod_linkedin module plugs into the
:ref:`authentication systen <guide-authentication>` to enable LinkedIn login
on your site.

Configuration
-------------

:ref:`Activate <activating-modules>` mod_facebook, then head to ‘Auth’ >
‘External services’ in the admin interface to enter your LinkedIn app ID and
secret. Enable LinkedIn login by checking the ‘Use LinkedIn authentication’ box.
This will add a ‘Log in with LinkedIn button to the logon form on your site.
